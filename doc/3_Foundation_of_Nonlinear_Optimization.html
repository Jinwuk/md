Convex Function

Definition : Convex function

f:Rn→R is said to be convex .
If ∀x,y∈Rn,λ∈[0,1], then

f(x+λ(y−x))≤(1−λ)f(x)+λf(y)

Theorem C-2

f is differentiable then f is convex iff ∀x,y∈Rn

f(y)−f(x)≥⟨∇f(x),(y−x)⟩

Remind : Differentiable

f(y)−f(x)=∫10∂f(x+s(y−x))∂x(y−x)ds

Proof of theorem C-2

Proof of Necessity (⇒)

Since f is convex i.e.

f(λx+(1−λ)y)≤λf(x)+(1−λ)f(y)

and f is differentiable i.e.

f(y)−f(x)=∫10⟨∇f(x+s(y−x)),y−x⟩ds

그러므로 x, y 사이의 한 점 λx+(1−λ)y,∀λ∈[0,1] 에 대하여

f(λx+(1−λ)y)−f(x)=∫10⟨∇f(x+s(λx+(1−λ)y−x))),(1−λ)(y−x)⟩ds=∫10⟨∇f(x+s(1−λ)(y−x)),(1−λ)(y−x)⟩ds=(1−λ)∫10⟨∇f(x+s(1−λ)(y−x)),(y−x)⟩ds

따라서 Convex 조건에서

f(λx+(1−λ)y)=f(x)+(1−λ)∫10⟨∇f(x+s(1−λ)(y−x)),(y−x)⟩ds≤λf(x)+(1−λ)f(y)

it implies that

(1−λ)∫10⟨∇f(x+s(1−λ)(y−x)),(y−x)⟩ds≤(1−λ)(f(y)−f(x))⇒∫10⟨∇f(x+s(1−λ)(y−x)),(y−x)⟩ds≤f(y)−f(x)

Let λ→1, then

∫10⟨∇f(x),(y−x)⟩ds≤f(y)−f(x)⇒⟨∇f(x),(y−x)⟩∫10ds≤f(y)−f(x)

Q.E.D of Necessity

Proof of Suffciency (⇐)

f(y)−f(x+λ(y−x))≥⟨∇f(x+λ(y−x)),(1−λ)(y−x)⟩(1)

f(x)−f(x+λ(y−x))≥⟨∇f(x+λ(y−x)),−λ(y−x)⟩(2)

(식 (2)의 경우 방향이 반대이므로 λ에 마이너스가 붙었다.)

(1) ×λ+ (2)×(1−λ)

λf(y)+(1−λ)f(x)−λf(x+λ(y−x))−(1−λ)f(x+λ(y−x))≥0⇒λf(y)+(1−λ)f(x)≥f(x+λ(y−x))

Theorem C-3

f is twice continuosly differentiable, then f is convex iff

∂2f(x)∂x2≥0,∀x∈Rn

∂2f(x)∂x2≥0,∀x∈Rn
